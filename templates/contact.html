What is Chessentials?

Chessentials is an AI-powered chess application that enhances your playing experience by letting you interact with Stockfish, one of the most powerful chess engines. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, Chessentials gives you a sleek interface for exploring different strategies, improving your skills, and having fun!

Flask Backend

The heart of Chessentials, built with the powerful and lightweight Flask framework for handling requests, routing, and game logic.
